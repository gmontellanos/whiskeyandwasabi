Sample Audit

Here’s an example of the kind of clarity and direction included in an audit.

Overview

Your content already has a calm, grounded identity. The biggest opportunity is not changing your style — it’s making your message clearer and more immediately felt.

What’s Working

Strong emotional tone, authentic pet moments, and a visual style that feels soft, trustworthy, and non-performative.

What’s Not Working Yet

Hooks can be too subtle, messaging shifts from post to post, and the content does not always make the viewer feel, “this is for me” within the first few seconds.

Audience Insight

The audience is not just “pet owners.” It is likely emotionally tired pet parents looking for comfort, softness, and realistic everyday companionship.

Content Direction

Lean further into low-energy pet care, cozy routines, quiet companionship, and gentle reassurance content with more repeatable hooks and clearer emotional targeting.
