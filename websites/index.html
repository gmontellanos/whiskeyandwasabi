The Process

Every project starts the same way — with questions that matter. I send a discovery survey before I design a single thing. It covers your brand, your audience, your competitors, and what success looks like for you. That's what makes the final result feel like yours.

① Discovery Survey

Before anything is designed, I send a survey covering your business, audience, aesthetic preferences, and goals. This is what one client called "simple yet poignant questions" — it's the foundation everything else is built on.

② Strategy + Planning

Based on your survey answers, I map out the site structure, page goals, and copy direction. Every page has a job — and we define it before the first line of code is written.

③ Design + Build

Custom design, coded from scratch. No templates, no drag-and-drop builders. Clean HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that loads fast and looks intentional on every screen size.

④ Review + Launch

You review the site, we make refinements, and then we launch. I handle domain connection, Google Analytics setup, and Search Console submission — so your site is findable from day one.
